## Top free courses

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Machine Learning (Stanford / Coursera)" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning">
    Andrew Ng's foundational ML course. Covers supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and practical advice. Free to audit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deep Learning Specialization (deeplearning.ai)" icon="brain" href="https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning">
    Five courses by Andrew Ng covering neural networks, CNNs, sequence models, and structuring ML projects. Free to audit; paid for certificates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intro to Deep Learning (MIT)" icon="graduation-cap" href="http://introtodeeplearning.com/">
    MIT's annual deep learning course. Lecture videos, slides, and labs are freely available.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Machine Learning Crash Course (Google)" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course/">
    Google's practical ML course using TensorFlow. Covers regression, classification, and neural networks with interactive exercises.
  </Card>
  <Card title="fast.ai" icon="rocket" href="https://www.fast.ai/">
    A top-down practical deep learning MOOC. Uses the fastai library on top of PyTorch. Highly regarded for accessibility and depth. Free.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CS231n: CNNs for Visual Recognition (Stanford)" icon="eye" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vT1JzLTH4G4&index=1&list=PL3FW7Lu3i5JvHM8ljYj-zLfQRF3EO8sYv">
    Stanford's seminal computer vision course. Spring 2017 iteration. Course website at cs231n.github.io. Free on YouTube.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Platform overview

| Platform | Focus | Pricing |
|---|---|---|
| [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org) | University-partnered courses and specializations | Free to audit; paid for certificates |
| [edX](https://www.edx.org) | University courses, professional certificates | Free to audit; paid for certificates |
| [Udacity](https://www.udacity.com) | Nanodegree programs with project reviews | Paid |
| [Udemy](https://www.udemy.com) | Individual courses, frequent sales | Paid (usually inexpensive) |
| [fast.ai](https://www.fast.ai) | Practical deep learning | Free |
| [Kaggle Learn](https://www.kaggle.com/learn) | Short practical courses with certificates | Free |
| [Cognitive Classes](https://cognitiveclass.ai) | IBM-backed courses in data science and AI | Free |
| [Dataquest](https://www.dataquest.io) | Data science in Python/R | Free and paid |
| [Manning](https://www.manning.com) | Books and live video courses | Paid |
| [DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai) | Andrew Ng's AI education platform | Free short courses; paid specializations |
