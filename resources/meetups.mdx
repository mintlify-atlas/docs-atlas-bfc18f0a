## Finding more meetups

The groups listed above represent verified active communities. To find additional meetups in your area:

- Search [Meetup.com](https://www.meetup.com) for "machine learning", "data science", or "artificial intelligence" filtered by your city.
- Check [Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com) for local and virtual ML events.
- Look for university ML reading groups and seminar series, which are often open to non-students.
- Follow local universities' ML or CS departments — many host public talks.

<Tip>
  If there is no active meetup in your area, starting one is straightforward. Meetup.com provides organizer tools, and an initial event with even 10–15 people can grow into an active community.
</Tip>
