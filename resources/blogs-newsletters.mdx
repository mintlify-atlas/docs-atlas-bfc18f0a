## General ML & data science blogs

A broad collection of active blogs covering data science, machine learning, and applied statistics.

- [Hacker News for Data Science — DataTau](https://www.datatau.com/news) — Community aggregator for data science links, similar to Hacker News.
- [freeCodeCamp Machine Learning Articles](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/tag/machine-learning/) — Tutorials and explainers on ML topics, free.
- [Distill](https://distill.pub) — Peer-reviewed research articles on machine learning presented with exceptional clarity and interactive visualizations.
- [Karpathy's Blog](https://karpathy.github.io/) / [Medium](https://medium.com/@karpathy) — Andrej Karpathy's writing on deep learning and AI.
- [Sebastian Raschka](https://sebastianraschka.com/) — Machine learning tutorials, book content, and practical guides.
- [Colah's Blog](https://colah.github.io/) — Highly visual explanations of neural network concepts, including LSTMs and attention.
- [Simply Statistics](https://simplystatistics.org/) — Statistics blog from Rafa Irizarry, Roger Peng, and Jeff Leek.
- [DataSchool](https://www.dataschool.io/) — Practical ML and data science tutorials by Kevin Markham.
- [Jake VdP's Blog](https://jakevdp.github.io/) — Jake VanderPlas's writing on Python, data science, and statistics.
- [Domino Data Lab Blog](https://blog.dominodatalab.com) — Data science in practice, tooling, and MLOps.
- [Kaggle Blog](http://blog.kaggle.com/) — Competition writeups, tutorials, and data science insights.
- [Count Bayesie](https://www.countbayesie.com) — Accessible Bayesian statistics for practitioners.
- [Randal Olson](http://www.randalolson.com/blog/) — Data visualization and machine learning.
- [Data Science Central](https://www.datasciencecentral.com) — Community hub for data science content.
- [Jeremy Kun's Blog](https://jeremykun.com/) — Math and programming, with ML and theory content.
- [iamtrask Blog](https://iamtrask.github.io/) — From-scratch neural network implementations.
- [Explain My Data](https://blog.explainmydata.com/) — Statistical methods and data analysis.
- [Statistical Modeling, Causal Inference (Andrew Gelman)](https://statmodeling.stat.columbia.edu) — Gelman's blog on Bayesian statistics and causal inference.
- [Evan Miller](https://www.evanmiller.org/) — Statistical methods and A/B testing.
- [nbshare.io Data Science Notebooks](https://www.nbshare.io/notebooks/data-science/) — Shared Jupyter notebooks for data science topics.
- [TrainInData Blog](https://www.blog.trainindata.com/) — ML engineering and feature engineering content.
- [Fennel Blog](https://fennel.ai/blog/) — Real-time feature engineering and ML infrastructure.
- [LightTag NLP Blog](https://www.lighttag.io/blog) — NLP and data annotation.
- [DataTalks.Club Articles](https://datatalks.club/articles.html) — Community-driven data science content.
- [Bugra's Blog](https://bugra.github.io/) — Signal processing, ML, and data science.
- [Peter Norvig / Newton.cx](https://newton.cx/~peter/) — Various writing on computing and AI.
- [West McKinney's Blog](http://wesmckinney.com) — Data tools and pandas.
- [DrewConway / Zia](http://drewconway.com/zia/) — Data science and network analysis.
- [Alexander Etz](https://alexanderetz.com/) — Bayesian statistics.
- [John Myles White](https://www.johnmyleswhite.com/) — Statistics and ML.
- [Overkill Analytics](https://www.overkillanalytics.net/) — Data science and analytics.
- [Hunch.net](http://hunch.net/) — ML theory by John Langford.
- [Shakir Mohamed's Blog](http://blog.shakirm.com/) — ML research and probabilistic modeling.
- [Yarin Gal's Blog](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/yarin.gal/website/blog.html) — Bayesian deep learning.
- [Dan Vak's Blog](https://www.danvk.org/) — Programming, data, and data visualization.
- [Ahmad Besbes](https://ahmedbesbes.com/) — Deep learning tutorials.
- [AI Summer](https://theaisummer.com/) — Deep learning and AI blog with articles and code examples.
