## Major ML & AI conferences

The following are the principal annual research conferences where state-of-the-art ML work is published. Proceedings from most of these conferences are freely available online.

### Core ML conferences

| Conference | Full name | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| NeurIPS | Neural Information Processing Systems | Broad ML and AI research |
| ICML | International Conference on Machine Learning | Core ML theory and algorithms |
| ICLR | International Conference on Learning Representations | Deep learning and representation learning |
| AISTATS | Artificial Intelligence and Statistics | Intersection of ML and statistics |
| UAI | Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence | Probabilistic and Bayesian methods |

### Computer vision

| Conference | Full name | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| CVPR | Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition | Computer vision |
| ICCV | International Conference on Computer Vision | Computer vision (biennial) |
| ECCV | European Conference on Computer Vision | Computer vision (biennial) |

### Natural language processing

| Conference | Full name | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| ACL | Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics | NLP and computational linguistics |
| EMNLP | Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing | Empirical NLP |
| NAACL | North American Chapter of the ACL | NLP in North America |

### Data mining & databases

| Conference | Full name | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| KDD | Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining | Data mining and applied ML |
| SIGMOD | Special Interest Group on Management of Data | Database systems and data management |
| VLDB | Very Large Data Bases | Large-scale data management |

### AI broadly

| Conference | Full name | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| AAAI | Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence | Broad AI research |
| IJCAI | International Joint Conference on AI | Broad AI |

## Where to find proceedings

Most conference papers are freely available:

- [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) — Papers linked with their code implementations.
- [arXiv cs.LG](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.LG/recent) — Machine learning preprints.
- [arXiv cs.CV](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.CV/recent) — Computer vision preprints.
- [arXiv cs.CL](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.CL/recent) — Computation and language preprints.
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) — AI-powered research paper search.
- [OpenReview](https://openreview.net/) — Open peer review for ICLR and other conferences.

<Note>
  Conference acceptance is highly competitive. NeurIPS, ICML, and ICLR typically have acceptance rates between 20–30%. Reading accepted papers from these venues is one of the most efficient ways to follow the state of the field.
</Note>
